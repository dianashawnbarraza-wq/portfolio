Executive Summary

Square Checking had strong product-market fit but underperformed on activation and ongoing engagement. Sellers opened accounts without understanding value, leading to low funding rates and shallow usage.

As Design DRI, I led a multi-quarter initiative to redesign onboarding, modernize checking IA across SPOS and web, and surface clearer account value at key moments. This case study walks through how we identified the opportunity, aligned cross-functional partners, and shipped measurable improvements.
