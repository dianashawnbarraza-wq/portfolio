11 years of designing for impact

I’m Diana (she/they), Senior Product Designer with 10+ years of experience building user-centered, scalable digital products across fintech and complex platforms. I specialize in product design for native mobile and web (UX/visual), product strategy, discovery, and new tech (AI prototyping and PR generation).

I bring a systems-level perspective to my work when collaborating with cross-functional partners and think deeply about the bigger picture when building customer-centered product. Previously a Banking and Payments design DRI at Square, I was responsible for design on Bill Pay (Square’s standalone AP automation payment tool), Square Checking engagement, and Cards (debit and Square Credit Card rewards).

I joined Square with a shared passion to provide economic empowerment to our sellers and I look forward to making a similar impact in my next role.

Open to conversations about product design, fintech, streamlining workflows, and building meaningful digital experiences.
